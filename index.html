SocialCircle

A speedy, lightweight and beautiful way to stay in contact with your best friends.

  • Own your database

    SocialCircle is on your server. You have the total control on your data.

  • It's open source

    The complete code is hosted on Github. You're free to help and contribute!

  • Fully customizable

    Change the theme or add a module, the only thing you need is css and/or php knowledge.

  • It runs on everything

    Access the app from your desktop computer or your mobile phone, you choose.

  • Super easy installation

    The installation is done in less than 5min. The longest part is to add people in your group!

  • Super extra mega light

    The whole app wheigs less than 100KB. That's not a lot.

  • Facebook-like chat

    Discuss with your friends with a useful simple chat.

  • Notifications

    Someone has commented your post? You'll get an email!

  • It's available for you too

    And if not (and you know a bit english), you can contribute by translate the system in your language.

  • Media-sharing

    It has never been so easy. Just drag'n'drop a media to publish it.

  • Events

    Create and manage events for your group.

  • Add users

    The only limit for your users is your server. You can have a 10-persons group like a 234374538-persons group (that's quite a lot).
